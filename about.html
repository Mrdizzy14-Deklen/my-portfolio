About Me

I'm currently pursuing a degree in Software Engineering at UT Dallas, with a strong focus on computer science and hands-on experience through various personal projects. My passion lies in game development, and I aspire to work in the industry, whether at a renowned company, as part of a small, innovative team, or by founding my own indie game studio.

In addition to my technical expertise, I strongly believe that games should be more than just entertainment—they should inspire and connect people. I’m passionate about creating immersive experiences that challenge players to think critically, engage with complex narratives, and explore new perspectives. I aim to push the boundaries of interactive storytelling and design, bringing fresh ideas to life. Whether I’m working on a personal project or collaborating with a team, my focus is always on innovation, inclusivity, and building experiences that leave a lasting impact.

I also value the importance of collaboration in game development. While I enjoy working on personal projects, I believe that some of the best ideas come from working alongside others who bring different perspectives and skills. Whether it’s brainstorming game mechanics, tackling challenging coding problems, or refining design elements, I thrive in environments where creativity and technical skills come together. Constant learning is a huge part of my journey, and I’m always eager to explore new tools, techniques, and ideas that can elevate my projects.
